Community Initiative for Enhanced Peace and Development domiciled Conflict Watch Center, which relies on various community based sources of information, and community specific indicators to generate analysis on potential conflict hotspots. The approach of the CWC is tuned towards predicting, tracking, monitoring and reporting on situations, events and actors (individual, group and institutional) with the capacity to cause violence. It is built on conflict preventive measures more than mitigating.
